Bean Soup

Description

This is a bean soup. I really like it.

It is very good if you are sick.

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Black-eyed peas
  • Olive Oil
  • Onions
  • Dill

Steps

  1. Cook onions in olive oil
  2. Add dill
  3. Add kale and black-eyed peas and vegetable stock
  4. Simmer for 2-3 hours
